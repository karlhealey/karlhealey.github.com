Karl Healey

 
 

The high-level goal of my research is to understand the mechanisms underlying human memory and how these mechanisms contribute to variations in memory ability. I am interested in variation both between different individuals (e.g., why some people do better than others on memory tests) and within individuals (e.g., why does a person’s memory change as they age).
