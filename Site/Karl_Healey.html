Research Interests

The overarching goal of my research program is to develop a computationally explicit theory of age-related and individual differences in memory and how this variation contributes to general intellectual ability. In pursuit of this goal I use a combination of behavioral experiments, computational modeling, computational statistics, and electrophysiological recordings.
